Robot Challenge workshop

The Robot Challenge workshop features several multi-disciplinary teams that join forces in specifying the behavior of an mBot. This behavior will help the mBot complete a predefined track with several obstacles. In various sprints, teams help the mBot to overcome each of the obstacles by collectively specifying behavior, implementing the behavior, and obtaining feedback. Finally, all teams engage in a competition: the team whose mBot completes the track quickest, wins eternal glory (and perhaps a nice price)
